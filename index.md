# Privacy Policy for Equinox                                                              
                                                                                            
**Last updated:** February 2026                                                           
                                                                                            
Equinox ("the App") is developed by Stefan Crafoord-Wiklund.                              
                                                                                            
## Information We Collect                                                                 
                                                                                            
### Location Data                                                                         
The App requests access to your device's location to calculate accurate sunrise and sunset times for your area. Your location data is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.                                                      
                                                                                        
### Saved Locations                                                                       
Any locations you manually save are stored locally on your device and are not shared with third parties.                                                                            
                                                                                            
## Advertising                                                                            
                                                                                            
The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to personalize ads according to their privacy policy. For more information, visit: (https://policies.google.com/privacy)                                     
                                                                                            
You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.                 
                                                                                            
## Data Sharing                                                                           
                                                                                            
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The App does not have user accounts and does not collect personal data beyond what is described above.                                                                          
                                                                                            
## Children's Privacy                                                                     
                                                                                            
The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
